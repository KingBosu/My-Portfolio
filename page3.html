About Me

My son and I

My name is William McKay, I am 31 years old and live in North Carolina. I grew up in the Pocono Mountains where I spent all of my childhood and the beginning of my adult life. After moving to North Carolina I was continually unsure about what I wanted to do next in life and have worked many different types of jobs such as

  • Security
  • Satellite Technician
  • Produce Associate
  • Mobile Retail Sales
  • Repair Technician
However through doing those things I learned that I was always seeking something that would make me feel more fulfilled. I have a decent amount of close friends many of whom are also into careers that deal with some type of technology and the lifestyles they tend to live seemed more like the one I desired for myself. Thus thrusting me into the direction of seeking to better myself in all ways that I could.
